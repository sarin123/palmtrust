About Palm Charitable Trust...

The group of young and dynamic IT and other professionals join together to serve humanity for the sake of Almighty. And formed/registered Palm Charitable Trust.

Palm Charitable Trust is working towards health and education as main focus areas, and is dedicated to work with Humanity. Irrespective of caste, creed, race, religion, and nationality.

We also partnered with or plan to partner with other trust and NGOs to help the humanity better in other areas of need like old age/orphanage homes. And social initiatives like awareness of child sex abuse prevention, training in various areas for life/skills development for underprivileged and so on.

Currently with the grace of god, We have been sponsoring around 250 dialysis per month for the Kidney failed patients with partnering with HBS Dialysis, a unit of HBS hospital., with support from like-minded donors.
